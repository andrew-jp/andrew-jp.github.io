Andrew Pritchett

I've got a lot of hobbies, including all the general Utah outdoor activites. I spend the warm months hiking, climbing, and working on math homework that I wish I'd taken in the fall. I spend the cold months skiing, snowboarding, gaming, and ice climbing.
