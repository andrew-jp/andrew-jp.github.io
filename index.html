Andrew Pritchett

My name is Andrew Pritchett. I was born in Stanford Hospital way back in 1995. After a few years in California, my family moved to Utah. Since then, I've spent most of my time here. I lived in Las Vegas for a few months last year, which was a nice change, but I moved back for ski season.

I've got a lot of hobbies, including all the general Utah outdoor activites. I spend the warm months hiking, climbing, and working on math homework that I wish I'd taken in the fall. I spend the cold months skiing, snowboarding, gaming, and ice climbing.
